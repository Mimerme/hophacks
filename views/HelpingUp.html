Helping Up Mission - For Men

This facility is an emergency housing service for men. Guests are introduced to The Helping Up Mission's Spiritual Recovery program (SRP). For more information contact The Helping Up Mission or visit the Helping Up Mission website.
There is an evening chapel message. If they realize a personal need for recovery, they may apply to our Spiritual Recovery Program.
